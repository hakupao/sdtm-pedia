## Convention Definition

**§2.12: H3 directly under H1 (no H2 intermediary)**

Trigger: A `###` heading appears directly under the file-root `#` heading with no `##` between them.

Rules:
- H3 HEADING atom: `parent_section = §D [D — Section Title]` (H1's namespace)
- H3 HEADING atom: `sibling_index = K` (1-based, H3-scope count under H1)
- Content atoms under H3: `parent_section = §D [D — H3 Title]` (H3 creates its own namespace)
- No `§D.N.K` suffix needed (no H2 title to reference); H3 anchors directly at H1 level

Distinction from §2.11 Plan B:
- §2.11: H3 under **numberless H2** → H3 namespace = `§D.N [H2 title]` + content `§D.N.K [H3 title]`
- §2.12: H3 under **H1** (no H2) → H3 namespace = `§D [D — file title]` + content `§D [D — H3 title]`
